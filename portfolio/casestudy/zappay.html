ZayPay

Secure payment experience that empowers users to send, receive, and manage money with confidence - anytime, anywhere.

Project Overview

This payment app redesign focuses on delivering a clean, modern, and user-friendly experience for managing everyday transactions. The goal was to create a visually appealing interface that ensures clarity and trust while performing secure payments. Starting from an engaging splash screen, the app guides users through smooth and intuitive flows for sending, receiving, and tracking money. Special attention was given to UI consistency, button clarity, and minimal form fields to reduce friction. The design emphasizes both aesthetics and function, aiming to make digital payments effortless for all users.

Project Statement

Many users find existing payment apps cluttered and confusing, especially during quick transactions. The lack of visual clarity, inconsistent layouts, and hidden action buttons often lead to errors or hesitation. Users also expect a secure, fast, and visually clean experience that builds trust with every payment.

Bio

Manu John is a 25-year-old freelance graphic designer who relies heavily on digital payment apps for both personal and professional use. he manages multiple transactions daily-from paying rent and utility bills to receiving payments from clients. With a busy schedule and frequent multitasking, he prefers apps that are fast, simple, and require minimal navigation. His main focus is on speed, transparency, and ease of use during every transaction.

Typography

Aa

I used inter as the primary typeface. Inter is a modern, highly readable sans-serif font designed especially for digital interfaces. It offers excellent clarity on all screen sizes, making it ideal for financial apps where precision and ease of reading are essential. Its geometric style and simple structure enhance the clean and professional look of the app.
